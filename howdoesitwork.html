How does it work How to Sell Your item

You want to sell your book ? Good.-- You will email us at augustanabooks@gmail.com stating the following elements: title of your book/supply, course number (if appliable), professor, your price, image of the book .

You will be notified with the drop off location once your item is sold-- the buyer should have your money, we guarantee that you will sell your book within a period ranging from one hour to one week.

How to Buy your item

First, you will need an augustana.edu email adress. After you browse our course categories you will choose your item, please remember that after you hit that buy button there is no coming back. You will be notified via email of the pick-up location, please have your money on hand, the price on the website is the only price you will have to pay there is no additionnal fees we are a free service.

IMPORTANT NOTE : If you fail to meet on the agreed appointment you will be deleted from our database, we will not be able to provide any future services.
